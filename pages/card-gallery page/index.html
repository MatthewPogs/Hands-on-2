The "Savage" or "Beast"

2012

Genre: Sports, Comedy, Drama

Player Name: Taiga Kagami

About: Taiga Kagami is the protagonist of Kuroko no Basket. He is a basketball prodigy who returns to Japan after living in the United States for several years.
